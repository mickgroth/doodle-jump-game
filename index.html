Doodle Jump

Use Arrow Keys or A/D to move

Jump on platforms and climb as high as you can!

🎯 Progressive Difficulty: New platform types unlock as you climb higher!

✨ Smooth physics: Accelerate, slide, and feel the momentum!

🌅 Dynamic Backgrounds: Journey from notebook paper → sky → sunset → night → space!

Controls: ← → or A D keys to move

Hold keys to accelerate, release to slide with momentum

🎯 SPACEBAR, W, or CLICK to shoot enemies!

⬇️ Jump on top of enemies to defeat them (Mario style!)

Platform Types (Progressive Difficulty):

Brown - Normal (stable) - Level 1+
Blue - Moving (slides left/right) - Level 2+ (Score 300+)
Tan - Breakable (breaks after one jump) - Level 3+ (Score 800+)
Purple - Disappearing (fades after touch) - Level 4+ (Score 1400+)

Power-Ups & Boosts:

🔴 Spring - Extra high jump! Found on platforms.
🔵 Jetpack - Rapid ascent for 3 seconds! (Score 100+)
🟠 Propeller Hat - Controlled flight for 4 seconds! (Score 100+)

⚔️ Enemies & Combat:

👾 Monsters - Jump on them or shoot them! (Score 150+)
🛸 UFOs - Jump on them or shoot them! (Score 150+)
⚠️ Warning: Touching enemies from the side = Game Over!
💫 Reward: +50 points per enemy (jump or shoot)
🛡️ Smart Placement: Enemies avoid spawning near springs to prevent unfair deaths!
